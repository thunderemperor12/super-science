About Us

Super Science Academy, Gadhinglaj, is dedicated to providing top-quality education to students from 8th to 12th grade. We specialize in preparing students for competitive exams like JEE (Mains & Advanced), NEET, MHT-CET, and other national and state-level exams.

Our Courses

  • Foundation Courses for 8th to 10th Grade
  • Science Stream Coaching for 11th and 12th Grade
  • JEE Mains and Advanced Preparation
  • NEET Coaching
  • MHT-CET Preparation
  • Other Competitive Exams
